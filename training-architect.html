Grooper "ACE" Training Program

To maximize client ownership of Grooper, a new training program dubbed Grooper A.C.E. (Architect – Consultant – Engineer) recently debuted through BIS. The three-tiered program is designed to give clients thorough knowledge of Grooper and its vast capabilities. The first group of Architect-level clients received their certifications in October and remarked at how comfortable they felt working within Grooper, as well as understanding ways to fully leverage the platform in their environment. Grooper A.C.E. training is a new approach in the industry with a goal of giving clients ultimate control over their data.

Level 1 - Architect Training

REQUIRED TEXTS AND MATERIALS

As of now, all required materials will be provided by BIS on site.

COURSE DESCRIPTION, GOALS, AND PREREQUISITE

This course is the first in a three-tiered certification program. Throughout the sequence, our goal is to develop a foundational basis with GROOPER. During this Architect tier training course, we will devote our attention to developing a general understanding of how and why GROOPER can do what it can do, and how you can begin to leverage it to affect your business goals. Course activities will be directed toward the development and assessment of the objectives listed in Architect Certification Objectives. By the end of this course you should be able to understand and create basic Batch Processes within GROOPER. You will demonstrate these skills via a variety of in-class activities, and a final assessment at the end of the course.

OVERVIEW/SCHEDULE OF TOPICS

  • DAY 1: Introductions, beginning concepts and general understanding of requirements and user interface
  • DAY 2: Further interaction with main components of Separation, Classification, and Extraction
  • DAY 3: Completion of GROOPER Phases 1-5, understanding, overview, and final assessment

FLUENCY OBJECTIVES

All class activities, and daily interactions are directed toward developing and assessing the following objectives.

  • You can confidently answer the question: What is GROOPER and how will it improve your business needs?
  • You understand the requirements of a GROOPER installation and means of deployment
  • You can setup a new GROOPER Repository and FileStore
  • You understand how to leverage GROOPER Services to automate tasks
  • You can explain the purposes of the different GROOPER applications
  • You can confidently navigate GROOPER Administration
  • You understand core vocabulary of GROOPER to differentiate unique objects and their purposes
  • You can explain the 5 Phases of GROOPER Process Engineering to effectively develop future Batch Processes

Training packages are available for licensed Grooper clients and certified Grooper partners.
